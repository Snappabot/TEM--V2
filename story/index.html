Our Story

Inspired by Spanish heritage and travels throughout Europe, Troweled Earth has been crafting innovative wall coatings for over two decades.

The Founder

José

José spent his younger years experimenting with the earth's many textures and forms before deciding to take his passion for natural materials to the next level. In 2002, he founded Troweled Earth, a company dedicated to creating high-quality wall art products that were both beautiful and sustainable.

Using natural beneficial plasters made with hemp, lime, clay and other sustainable materials, Troweled Earth products were designed to improve the quality of homes and buildings, whilst also providing an attractive and unique design element.

The Director

Jarrad

A few years after José established the company, Jarrad brought a new level of energy to the business. With a background in commerce, marketing and plastering, Jarrad was able to help Troweled Earth expand its reach, influence, and portfolio.

Jarrad wears many hats in the company, from product developer to sales and marketing manager, and has been instrumental in helping the company grow and succeed.

Melbourne Director

Matt

One of the newest yet most experienced members to join the team is Matt. Matt came on board in 2018 and it was soon clear that he shared the same vision for the Troweled Earth brand as José and Jarrad.

With over 20 years of plastering experience and running his own business, Matt has a goal to help grow the company and take its reach worldwide.

The New Chapter

Troweled Earth Melbourne

After a successful run of over 20 years in Western Australia, the Troweled Earth team made the decision to bring their expertise to the East Coast. Troweled Earth Melbourne, headed by Matt, provides product sales, product training and customised product application through certified Troweled Earth trained applicators.

The Troweled Earth team are passionate about their work, and they are dedicated to creating high-quality, sustainable products that will make a positive impact on the environment and the people who use them.
