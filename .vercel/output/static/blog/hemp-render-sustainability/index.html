Safety and Sustainability of Earthen Hemp Rammed Render

Our Earthen Hemp Rammed Render achieves fire safety certification while delivering exceptional environmental credentials. Discover how natural building can meet modern standards.

Building Naturally, Building Safely

There’s a misconception that natural building materials can’t meet modern safety standards. Our Earthen Hemp Rammed Render proves otherwise — achieving rigorous fire safety certification while offering environmental benefits that synthetic alternatives simply cannot match.

Fire Safety Certification

After extensive testing, we’re proud to announce that our Earthen Hemp Rammed Render has achieved fire certification compliant with Australian building standards. This means:

  • Approved for use in residential and commercial applications
  • Meets BCA requirements for wall systems
  • Documented fire resistance for peace of mind

This certification opens doors for architects and builders who want to specify sustainable materials without compromising on safety compliance.

What Makes Hemp Render Different?

Unlike conventional renders that rely on cement and synthetic additives, our hemp render combines:

Hemp Shiv
The woody core of the hemp plant, processed into lightweight aggregate. Hemp is:

  • A fast-growing, renewable crop
  • Carbon-negative (absorbs more CO2 than it releases)
  • Naturally resistant to pests and mould

Natural Lime Binder
Lime has been used in construction for millennia. It:

  • Absorbs CO2 as it cures
  • Remains breathable throughout its lifespan
  • Can be recycled at end of life

Earth & Natural Pigments
Local clays and mineral pigments provide colour and character without synthetic dyes.

Performance Benefits

Sustainability doesn’t mean sacrifice. Hemp render delivers practical advantages:

Thermal Regulation
The combination of hemp’s insulating properties and lime’s thermal mass creates walls that buffer temperature extremes naturally — reducing heating and cooling costs.

Moisture Management
Breathable walls prevent the moisture trapping that causes mould in sealed modern buildings. The wall “breathes,” maintaining healthy indoor humidity levels.

Acoustic Performance
The fibrous structure provides excellent sound absorption, creating quieter, more comfortable spaces.

Durability
Lime renders gain strength over time as they continue to carbonate. Properly maintained, they can last centuries.
