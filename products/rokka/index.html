Extremely durable micro cement with high cement content for a variety of stunning finishes.

Features

Key Features

  • Thickness: 2-5mm application
  • Extremely durable - high cement content
  • Variety of finishes: pitted, smooth, or sand finish
  • Generous working time - can be applied over a longer period
  • Re-mixable - can be re-mixed over the course of the day
  • Less shrinkage issues - more stable and resistant to cracking
